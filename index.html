Privacy Policy

Guruhelper (Ajlyn Huvaari)
Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Guruhelper ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name and email address when you register or sign in using email, Google, or Apple.
  • User Content: Photos, images, and audio recordings that you attach to your tasks and schedules.
  • User Identifiers: A unique user ID is generated for your account to provide personalized services.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens to send you notifications about your tasks.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality (scheduling, task management)
  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To send push notifications about your tasks and team activities
  • To enable team collaboration features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Cloud Firestore for storing your schedules and tasks
  • Firebase Cloud Storage for storing your photos and audio files

All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only shared within your team if you choose to join or create a team within the app.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase - for authentication, database, and file storage
  • Google Sign-In - for optional Google account authentication
  • Apple Sign-In - for optional Apple account authentication

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Update or correct your personal information through the app's profile settings

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
